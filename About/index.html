ABOUT US

Discover a hotel that
defines
a new dimension of luxury.

The story of Galadari Hotel Colombo which opened its doors in 1984 is a
splendid tale of continual improvement of product and the highest
standards of quality in hospitality.

Having embraced over 3 decades of expertise in hospitality our vision and
beliefs are firmly grounded in extending a true personalized service to all
our guests, laced with an unforgettable luxury hotel experience.

The brand has enticed many elite personalities from around the world
including heads of government, prime ministers of leading nations,
royalty, well known sports and entertainment personalities and many
more.

Galle Face Green A wide expanse of green facing the Indian Ocean that is ideal for picnics and other outdoor activities
Dutch Hospital Coincided to be the oldest building in the Colombo Fort area dating back to the Dutch colonial era. It is now a heritage building and a shopping and dining precinct.
Royal Colombo Golf Club Second oldest Royal Golf Club outside the United Kingdom which was founded over 130 years ago
Independence Square A national monument built for commemoration of the independence of Sri Lanka reflecting distinct Sri Lankan architecture
National Museum A rich reservoir of ancient relics dating back when texts were written on palm leaves
Pettah A commercial district with streets well-known for selling clothing, electronics, and limitless knickknacks at very affordable prices
The Fort Housing offices, shops and hotels, some of which exuding old world charm with colonial architecture
Gangarama Temple A picturesque temple comprising the best of architecture which has been in existence for over 120 years
Viharamahadevi Park The city's largest park, luring visitors with its beautiful landscape
Kelaniya Raja Maha Vihara A famous Buddhist temple which serves as a venue for a religious procession
